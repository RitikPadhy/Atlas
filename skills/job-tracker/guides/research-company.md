# Research a company

Goal: a useful brief before applying or interviewing, saved for reuse.

## Steps
1. `web_search` the company: what they do, recent news, funding/size, products,
   and the team/role if known. `web_fetch` the most useful results (their site,
   a recent article, the careers page).
2. Synthesize a brief:
   - What they do (1–2 lines) and how they make money.
   - Recent news / trajectory (last 6–12 months).
   - Why it could fit the user (tie to `config/profile.md`).
   - 2–3 smart questions the user could ask in an interview.
   - Any red/yellow flags (layoffs, reviews, instability) — note honestly with sources.
3. Save it with `write_file` to `companies/<company>.md` so it's reused for interview prep.
4. Cite source URLs.

## Notes
- Distinguish verified facts (with a source) from inference. Don't fabricate news.
