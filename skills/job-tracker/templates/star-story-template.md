# STAR Stories

Reusable interview stories. One per heading. The agent matches these to behavioural
questions during interview prep. Keep each tight and metric-driven.

## <Short title — e.g. "Cut deploy time 60%">
- **Situation:** <context — where, when, what was at stake>
- **Task:** <what you specifically needed to do>
- **Action:** <the concrete steps YOU took>
- **Result:** <outcome with a number; what you learned>
- **Good for questions about:** <leadership / conflict / failure / impact / ownership>
